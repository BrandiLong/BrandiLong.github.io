Cake Match Puzzle

Overview

How to Play

  1. Drag and drop slices onto the tray.
  2. Matching slices merge into larger pieces. Get six slices together to make a full cake!
  3. Full cakes disappear, freeing up space for more slices.
  4. Watch out for mixed plates with slices from two different cakes—they’ll test your skills!
  5. If the tray fills up, it’s game over.
  6. The more cakes you complete, the more coins you earn. Use those coins to unlock upgrades, adding cool toppings and decorations to your cakes.
