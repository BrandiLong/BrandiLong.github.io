    • Ways To Give

    At the Weld Food Bank there are three ways to give: time, money, and food. All are needed to help to make a difference in our community where 1 out of 4 people go hungry. This means around 88,000 people in our community are needing food assistance. 26,000 of that number is children in need as 1 out of 3 children need food assistance. The Weld Food Bank wants to serve those needing food asssistance in Weld County through various methods including:

  • The Mobile Food Pantry
  • Kids Summer Backpacks Program
  • Senior Feeding Program
  • School food pantries
  • Emergency Food Program
  • Farms to Families
  • Kids Cafe After School Program
